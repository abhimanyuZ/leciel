Location:

NITPY is situated in Karaikal. NITPY is located on the National Highway NH32 at about 58 Kms from Chidambaram towards South. The Institute is located about 8.5 Kms from Karaikal Bus stand on the Nagapattinam to Chennai Highway (NH 32). Karaikal Railway Station is about 11 Kms from NITPY.
